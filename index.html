THE JEWEL GAME

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number shown to you, you lose the game if your total score exceeds this number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you, until you click on the crystal.

At the beginning of each game the crystals will be assigned new random values.
